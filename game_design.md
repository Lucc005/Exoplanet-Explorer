# Exoplanet Explorer

## Inspiration

A retro pixel-art adventure inspired by Pokémon and classic space games.

## Story

The player is humanity's newest explorer. Their mission is to travel across five different exoplanets, defeat alien invaders, build a stronger rocket, and become an official Exoplanet Explorer.

## Planet Order

1. Super-Earth 🌍
2. Sub-Neptune 🌊
3. Gas Giant 🪐
4. Hot Jupiter 🔥
5. Ice Planet ❄️

## Player Progression

Weapon upgrades after every planet.

Rocket upgrades after every planet.

Plant a personalized flag after liberating each world.

Receive an Exoplanet Explorer Certificate at the end.
